Game Rules:

  • Each NFT rarity has a different amount of Bullets, Time, and Max Score.
  • Higher rarity NFTs earn more rewards than lower rarity ones.
  • If your Bullets reach zero, the game is over.
  • If your Time runs out, the game is over.
  • If you reach your Max Score, the game ends.
  • Shoot zombies to earn rewards—each score grants 2 $CGUNs.
  • Use the mouse to aim and left-click to shoot.
  • Each zombie killed will earn you 2 $CGUNs.
  • Have fun, and good luck!
